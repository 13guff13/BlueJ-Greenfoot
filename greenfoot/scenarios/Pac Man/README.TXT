USER INSTRUCTIONS:
Control Pac Man to move around
the level and collect food pills.
Controls are the arrow keys or
the w,a,s and d keys for Pac Man's movement.
Collect an Energizer pill to turn the Ghosts blue,
and when they are blue Pac Man can eat them.
If you touch a Ghost when they are normal,
they will kill Pac Man.
